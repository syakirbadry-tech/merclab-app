# ⚡ Quick Deploy Guide - 3 Steps to Live

## Step 1️⃣: Choose Your Hosting

**Easiest:** GitHub Pages (free)  
**Fastest:** Vercel (free, super fast)  
**Flexible:** Netlify (free, custom domain)

Pick one ↓

---

## Step 2️⃣: Upload Files

All your files are in the `/outputs` folder:
```
index.html
manifest.json
service-worker.js
icons/ (folder with all icon sizes)
```

Upload these to your chosen platform.

---

## Step 3️⃣: Get Your Live URL

Once deployed, you'll get a URL like:
- GitHub: `https://your-username.github.io/merclab-app`
- Netlify: `https://your-app-name.netlify.app`
- Vercel: `https://your-app-name.vercel.app`

---

## 📱 Install on iPhone

1. Open Safari → go to your URL
2. Tap Share (↗️)
3. Tap **Add to Home Screen**
4. Tap **Add**
5. Done! Your app is on your home screen 🎉
